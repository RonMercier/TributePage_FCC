Michael Jordan

The Greatest of All Time

Timeline of how the man became the Legend:

  • 1963 - Michael Jordan was born on February 17 in Brooklyn, New York. His family moved to North Carolina shortly after he was born.
  • 1978 - Jordan attended Laney High School where he averaged 25 points per game.
  • 1981 - Jordan gets a basketball scholarship to the University of North Carolina.
  • 1982 - Michael makes the game winning shot against Georgetown to win the NCAA National Title.
  • 1984 - Jordan played on the US Men's Olympic Team where he won an Olympic gold medal, and he is also drafted by the Chicago Bulls.
  • 1986 - He set the still unbroken record for points in a playoff game with 63 points against the Boston Celtics.
  • 1987 - He became the only player to score 3000 points in a NBA season.
  • 1988 - Won his first NBA Most Valuable Player award and was named NBA's Defensive player of the Year.
  • 1989 - He led the league in scoring an average 32.5 points per game.
  • 1990 - The Chicago Bulls reach the Conference Finals where they lost to the Detroit Pistons.
  • 1991 - Won his second MVP award and won the NBA Championship against the Los Angeles Lakers.
  • 1992 - Jordan led the US Olympic Basketball team to the gold medal against Croatia. The Chicago Bulls win another NBA Championship.
  • 1993 - The Bulls win their third NBA Championship against the Phoenix Suns.
  • 1996 - Made the list of the 50 Greatest player in NBA history. He also leads the Bulls to their fourth NBA Championship, after leading them to a 72-10 regualar season record.
  • 1997 - The Bulls win their 5th NBA title against the Utah Jazz.
  • 1998 - The Chicago Bulls win their sixth NBA title against the Utah Jazz.
  • 1999 - He retired for the second time and named the greatest North American Athlete of the 20th century.
  • 2000 - He became part owner of the Washigton Wizards and became their President of Basketball Operations.
  • 2001 - Jordan came out of retirement to play for the Washington Wizards.
  • 2002 - He suffers a knee injury that ended his season.
  • 2003 - Jordan passes Kareem Abdul-Jabbar as the all-time leading scorer in All-Star game history. He also became the first 40 year old to score 43 points.
  • 2006 - He becomes part owner of the now Charlotte Hornets.
  • 2009 - Michael Jordan was inducted into the Basketball Hall of Fame.
  • 2010 - Jordan was ranked one of the 20th Most Powerful Celebrity by Forbes Magazine.

Aside from winning numerous championships and accolades, Michael jordan has also starred in a successful movie called Space Jam, has a successful restaurant business, and was the face of a number of brands such as Nike, Hanes, Gatorade, and Wheaties. Also, from 2001 - 2014 Jordan has hosted an annual charitable golf event with the proceeds going to foundations including Make-A-Wish, Cats Care, the James R. Jordan Foundation, Keep Memory Alive and Opportunity Village.
